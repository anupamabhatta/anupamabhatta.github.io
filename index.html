About Me

Hello! I am a first-year graduate student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I am pursuing a Master of Science in Information (MSI), with a specialization in data science.
Before embarking on this exciting academic journey, I worked as a Senior Solutions Analyst at Loyal for three years, where I spearheaded the management and enhancement of listings data, ensuring the dissemination of accurate and comprehensive information across various third party listing management platforms including Google, Bing, Apple, Facebook, and Foursquare.

As a dedicated problem-solver, I find fulfillment in leveraging my data science skills to uncover actionable insights, collaborate cross-functionally, and determine innovative solutions that drive progress. My passion is utilizing analytical thinking and precision to unravel complex challenges and enable data-driven decision-making that solves multifaceted problems.
For a deeper dive into my skills and projects, I invite you to explore my GitHub profile. Let's connect and explore the boundless possibilities that data science has to offer!
